Welcome to the Usability Evaluation

Thank you for participating! This study evaluates the usability of our web platform for the ultrasound assessment of adnexal masses.

Instructions

  1. Log in with the test credentials provided.
  2. Complete the questionnaire for Patient A (no mass).
  3. Complete the questionnaire for Patient B (with mass), generate ECO-SCORE, and download the report.
  4. Add a simulated histopathology result for Patient B.
  5. Check the list of questionnaires and export data as CSV.
  6. Log out securely.
